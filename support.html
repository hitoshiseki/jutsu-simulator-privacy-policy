Frequently asked questions

How do I trigger a technique?

Each technique has its own gesture — tap, shake, throw, or blow into the microphone. The in-app tutorial for each technique shows you exactly what to do.

A technique won't activate with motion or microphone. What now?

Make sure you granted the Motion and Microphone permissions when prompted. You can re-enable them in your device Settings under the app's permissions.

How do I remove ads / unlock premium?

Open the app and choose the ad-free / premium upgrade. Purchases are handled by the App Store or Google Play.

I bought the upgrade but it's not showing. How do I restore it?

Use the "Restore Purchases" option in the app. If it still doesn't appear, email us with the store account you purchased from and we'll help.

How do I switch language?

The app supports English and Portuguese. Use the language toggle in the app; your choice is saved automatically.

Is my microphone audio recorded?

No. The microphone is only used to detect blowing in real time. Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. See the privacy policy for details.
